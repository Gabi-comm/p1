# About RivanCyber

**Cisco • CompTIA • CCNA • CCNP • Fullstack** *Flexible Weekend & Evening Schedules | Industry-Standard Portfolio Projects*

RivanCyber Training Institute, Inc. is a premier IT training provider based in the Philippines. Established in the year 2000, the institute has built a twenty-six-year reputation for excellence in delivering hands-on, mentor-led IT education. 

We specialize in transforming ambitious beginners into certified professionals and helping seasoned IT specialists upscale their careers to meet global standards.

---

### 🎯 Mission
To provide "zero-to-hero" IT training that bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application. We focus on job-ready skills, equipping students and professionals with the expertise needed to excel in networking, cybersecurity, and modern software development.

### 👁️ Vision
To be the leading catalyst for tech talent acceleration in Southeast Asia, recognized for producing highly skilled, certified, and globally competitive IT professionals who drive digital transformation.

---

### 📈 Our Impact
* **Established:** Since 2000 (26 Years of Excellence)
* **Learners Trained:** 20,000+ Alumni globally
* **Certification Pass Rate:** 99% *(for students who completed training and sat the exam)*
* **Career Growth:** Thousands of alumni now working in top-tier tech firms, telcos, and multinational corporations.

* **Flexible, Career-Friendly Schedules:** Designed specifically for working professionals, busy students, and career-switchers, offering comprehensive weekend and evening class options.
