# Training Programs and Courses

Rivan offers specialized training tracks designed to equip students with job-ready technical skills.

### Core Training Tracks
*   **Networking**
*   **Cybersecurity**
*   **Cloud**
*   **Enterprise I.T.**

### Certification Preparation
Programs include effective reviewers and dedicated practical labs optimized for certification preparation. 
*   **CompTIA Courses:** Students enrolling in CompTIA courses are eligible for discounted exam vouchers.

### Class Policies & Availability
*   **Batch Limitations:** To ensure a quality hands-on experience, there are limited slots available per batch. 
*   **Reservations:** Interested participants are encouraged to send a message to inquire about schedules, training programs, and reservations.
